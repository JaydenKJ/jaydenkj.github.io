Sunset

The sky ignites in hues of gold,
As day’s soft whispers start to fold,
In twilight’s tender, warm embrace,
The sun dips low, a fleeting grace. A canvas brushed with fiery light,
Streaked with crimson, pink, and white,
The horizon’s edge aglow with fire,
As day and night in dance conspire. The tranquil sea reflects the blaze,
In shimmering, liquid waves of praise,
While shadows stretch and colors blend,
A fleeting moment’s day’s own end. The sun descends in regal might,
Surrendering to the coming night,
A gentle hush upon the land,
As dusk’s soft fingers brush the sand. Each evening’s close a quiet song,
Of light and dark where dreams belong,
A symphony of peace and rest,
The sunset’s gift, forever blessed.
